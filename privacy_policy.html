Privacy Policy for RF Field Hunter

Effective date: 2026-07-03

RF Field Hunter is an offline-first Android application by Crossfade RF Labs for RF field mapping, direction marking, GPS/map workflows and optional RTL-SDR receiver use.

Summary

RF Field Hunter does not require a user account, does not show ads and does not use a central RF server in this release.

The app is designed to keep field work local on the user's device. Location and field marker information are used for app functionality such as map display, compass/bearing workflows and manual field pins.

Information the app uses

  • Device location, when you grant location permission, to show your current position on the map and support bearing/field navigation features.
  • Compass and device sensor data to display heading and support direction-marking workflows.
  • Locally created field pins, bearings and notes entered by the user.
  • Network access for online map tiles and optional SDR-related local/network communication.
  • Optional SDR receiver data when a compatible RTL-SDR device and a separate SDR Driver app are used.

Information collected by the developer

In this release, RF Field Hunter does not send your GPS location, field pins, bearings, SDR data, RF reports, notes or account information to a developer-operated central RF server.

The developer does not operate a user account system for this app release and does not receive user-submitted field reports from the app.

Third-party services and external apps

RF Field Hunter may request online map tiles from map providers when online map layers are used. These providers may receive technical information normally included in web requests, such as IP address, approximate network location, device/browser/app request metadata and requested map tile coordinates.

Optional SDR receiver mode depends on a separate third-party SDR Driver app. That driver app is separate from RF Field Hunter and is governed by its own privacy practices and permissions.

Data sharing

RF Field Hunter does not sell user data.

RF Field Hunter does not share user-created field pins, bearings, notes, GPS location or RF reports with a developer-operated server in this release.

Data storage

Field pins, bearing information and settings may be stored locally on your device so the app can provide its field workflow features. Removing the app or clearing app storage may remove locally stored app data, depending on Android system behavior and device settings.

Permissions

  • Location permission: to show your position and support map/bearing features.
  • Internet/network access: to load online map tiles and support optional local/network SDR workflows.

Children

RF Field Hunter is not designed for children. The Google Play listing is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as RF Field Hunter changes. Any future release that adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, ads or a central server will require an updated privacy policy and updated Google Play Data Safety disclosure.
